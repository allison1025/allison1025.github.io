hello, nice to meet you!!

I’m Allison Yih: a San Francisco-based data scientist and vegan foodie. I'm passionate about using big data analytics to extract insights that answer society’s lingering questions.

Before jumping into the world of big data, I studied viola for a decade. During this time, I’ve realized each piece has a story that must be woven together. To achieve that, I began to deeply interpret the relationships between the notes and the musical symbols to craft the story they tell together.

Through my music background, I’ve learned to value the power of storytelling in my technical career. Like expressing the unwritten emotions in each measure of music, I believe in synthesizing seemingly-different information (numbers with qualitative research) to effectively communicate cohesive insights behind the data, which others can utilize to make well-informed decisions.
